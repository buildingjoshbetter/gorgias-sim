// 01 — The gap

Everyone models elections.
Nobody models the bill.

A legislator can poll a district to the decimal. But no tool shows the second-order consequences of a law before it is written — the statutes it breaks, the seniors it over-taxes, the half-billion it costs. One line in becomes hundreds of nodes waking up.
